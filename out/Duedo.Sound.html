Class: Sound

Sound(source, name, volume, soundManager, connect)

Create a new sound object. The events that can be binded (sound.Bind(eventName, func)) to the sound are: ended, resume, play, volume, stop, pause. With the introduction of the new google policies, the sounds are played only after the user has interacted, even with just a click, with the page.
